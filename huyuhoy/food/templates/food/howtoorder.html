How to Order Online

Visit our website and browse our menu to discover a variety of delicious food options


Orders may be placed online for:


  • Deliver – Have your orders delivered as soon as possible to your preferred address.

  • Pick-Up – Order in advance then pick up at HUYUHOY store at your most convenient time.

    1. Create an Account
      To place an order, you can either create an account or proceed as a guest. Creating an account allows you to save your preferences, track your order history, and receive special offers.

    2. Add Items to Your Cart
      Select the items you wish to order and add them to your cart. You can review your selections and make adjustments before proceeding to checkout.

    3. Provide Delivery Information
      Enter the delivery address and any specific delivery instructions if needed. Ensure the accuracy of the information to facilitate a smooth delivery process

    4. Review Your Order
      Before confirming your order, review the items in your cart, delivery details, and total cost. You can make changes or add special instructions at this stage.

    5. Payment
      Choose your preferred payment method and provide the necessary payment information. Your payment information is securely processed by our trusted payment service providers.

    6. Place Your Order
      Click the "Proceed" button to finalize your order. You will receive an order confirmation via email, which includes the estimated delivery time.
